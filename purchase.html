Formal Negotiation

Over 2,532 copies sold!

Rating: 4.3

Author: Jeremy Ymerej

Published: September 10, 2003

Publisher: Stone ridge publishing co.

Book description

In a world where power is a game of leverage, Formal Negotiation delves into the intricacies of deals, betrayals, and the art of persuasion.

When corporate strategist Elise Mercer is thrust into a high-stakes merger between two rival tech empires, she quickly realizes this is no ordinary negotiation. The boardroom becomes a battlefield, and Elise must navigate hidden agendas, buried secrets, and volatile alliances. As tensions escalate, what begins as a professional challenge turns personal, exposing the fragility of trust in a cutthroat world.

Behind closed doors, the stakes extend beyond profit margins. A mysterious player seems determined to sabotage the deal, and Elise finds herself questioning her loyalties—and her limits.

Formal Negotiation is a gripping tale of power dynamics, ambition, and the delicate balance between risk and reward, perfect for fans of sharp, suspenseful corporate drama.
